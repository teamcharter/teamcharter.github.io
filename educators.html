How It Works


Set Expectations

Charter empowers students to decide on ground rules and member responsibilities from the start.

Many students don't set expectations because they don't want to be seen as overbearing.
By giving your students Charter, you give them permission to have frank discussions about how to operate as team.

Identify Struggling Teams

Charter teaches students about deliverables and milestones, so they can produce higher quality work.

You can track teams in your dashboard to see who is contributing, who isn't, and why.

Assess Team Performance

Using our collaboration analytics, you can assess teams as a whole or as individuals in order to give more fair grades.
